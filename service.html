Keepers of the Covenant

I chaired the Keepers of the Covenant from 2017-2021. The Keepers of the Covenant is a group of faculty, staff, and students whose directive is to keep the Maryville College Covenant as a vital part of the community and its standards. For information on the group and the MC Covenant please see the following link: (MC Covenant). Two primary events we hold each year are the New Student Covenant Ceremony and the Spirit of the Covenant Award.

During my first 2 years on Keepers, we worked with a talented Student Ryan Lay to create informational videos on each of the 3 key tenats of the MC Covenant. In addition, we also created a clip explaining the meaning of the Spirit of the Covenant Award. Click on "Video Project from Keepers" to see these on the MC Offical Website.

In 2018, WVLT did a report on our MC Covenant. The photo journalist covering the ceremony did a wonderful job memorlizing the event.

Additional Service to Maryville College

  1. Elected representative of Mathematics and Natural Sciences Divisions to Maryville College Academic Life Council (ALC). On ALC, I served as recorder. 2018-2021.
  2. Advisor of the Student Organization on 3D Printing "MC3D". Fall 2018 to present.
  3. Creation of Makerspace for MC. This has included donating four 3Dprinters and assisting in the maintence and running of these machines.
  4. Participated in “Teaching Ethical CitizenshipTraining” during the Spring of 2018. Taught Ethics 490 Senior Capstone course at MC since the Spring of 2019.
  5. Led research on creation of BS and modified BA degree in Mathematics at Maryville College (Fall 2017) which was approved during the 2017/2018 Academic Year.
  6. Directed a Scots Science Scholars research project “Synchronicity among Gender-Identifying Pairs and Quantifying the Complexity of Rectilinear Motion.” This work as presented by students at the Joint Mathematics Meeting in 2016/2017.
  7. Created and graded exams in the Comprehnsive Exams at MC since 2017. I currently organize and supervise these exams for the Division of Mathematics and Computer Science. This work included the creation of a Tartan site with resoruces for students.
  8. Support of the division at Meet Maryville and Great Beginning Events. I also supported our local ACM chapter by helping set up Retro Game Night Events.
  9. Service as an academic advisor to both Mathematics and Computer Science students. I also serve as a senior study advisor having between 1 to 2 studies active at a time. I have had the please of advising two exemplary senior studies.

MC Cares

During the Covid-19 Pandemic, I and collegue Dr. Chase Worley put our 3D printers to work to do good on the largest possible scale.

My interest in the field of 3D printing started because of the MC Covenant. When I was a new member of Keepers, I followed through with the idea of 3D printing a copy of the MC Covenant Stone. This was my very first 3D project and what started my hobby of 3D printing. The original model for the Covenant Stone was a scan by a member of our IT department (Tobby Ryan).

As an alumnus of Maryville College, it is also because of the MC Covenant that I am excited to help make face shields for Blount Memorial Hospital. Putting my knowledge of 3D printing into a force for good is respecting and supporting my neighbors. I think we are all called to help others when we can, and we should put action to our words – that’s integrity. I’m thankful for Dr. Chase Worley getting the ball rolling on this project. The work was done during the Spring of 2020.
